TRELLIS TUTOR AGREEMENT


The Organization is of the opinion that the Tutor has the necessary qualifications, experience and abilities to assist and benefit the Organization in its operation.

The Tutor accepts the terms and conditions set out in this Agreement by ticking the checkbox and clicking the 'I AGREE' button below, as an equivalent to the tutor's legal signature.

In consideration of the matters described above and of the mutual benefits and obligations set forth in this Agreement, the receipt and sufficiency of which consideration is hereby acknowledged, the parties to this Agreement agree as follows:

Tutor Duties and Descriptions
1. The job duties the Tutor is expected to perform will be the following:

Fill-up required forms, coordinate with Tutor’s page set-up, attend initial training and pass the actual evaluation prior the online publishing of the Tutor’s page. Once a Tutor’s page was published, the Tutor is required to regularly monitor all tutee/parent inquiries, booking appointments and rescheduling.  Tutor is also required to maintain a Level 3 performance. For Levels 1 to 2 performance, Tutor is required to undergo a refresher in-house course-training.

 The Tutor accepts to be the subject of, the general supervisions of, and act pursuant to the orders, advice and direction of the Organization.

 The Tutor will perform all duties requested by the Organization that are reasonable and that are customarily performed by a person holding a similar position in the industry or operation of the Organization.

 The Organization may make changes to the Tutor duties where the changes would be considered reasonable for a similar position in the industry of operation of the Organization. The Tutor’s duties may be changed by agreement and with the approval of both the Tutor and the Organization or after a notice period required under law.

 The Tutor agrees to abide by the Organization’s rules, regulations, and practices, including those concerning task accountability & information credibility, as they may from time to time be adopted or modified.

Tutor Compensation
 Compensation paid to the Tutor for the services rendered by the Tutor as required by this Agreement (the “Compensation”) will include a 65% commission for every service rendered of a Level 1 to 4 Tutors, while a Level 5 Tutor is compensated with a 90% commission.

 The Tutor’s level is determined by the tutee’s/parent’s rating submitted after the tutorial session was completed. In case the tutee/parent was not able to submit their attendance and rating, a 2-star rating will be given automatically.

 The Tutor’s compensation is released or liquidated only after a complete and successful tutorial session was accomplished which is verified by the parent’s submission of the “Submit Attendance” form. 

In case of failure of the parents to submit the “Submit Attendance” form, the Tutor’s compensation is released or liquidated after; 4 days for a Week Tutorial Service; or 3 weeks for a Month Tutorial Service.

 Non-Solicitation
  The Tutor understands and agrees that any attempt on the part of the Tutor to induce other tutors, contractors, tutee and tutee parents, to leave the Organization, or any effort by the Tutor to interfere with the Organization’s relationship with its other tutors, contractors, tutee and tutee parents would be harmful and damaging to the Organization. The Tutor agrees that during the Tutor’s term of service with the Organization and for a period of (1) year after the end of that term, the Tutor will not in any way, directly or indirectly:  Induce or attempt to induce any tutors, contractors, tutee and tutee parents  of the Organization to quit their subscription or service with the Organization;

b. Otherwise interfere with or disrupt the Organization’s relationship with its tutors, contractors, tutee and tutee parents.

c. Discuss organizational opportunities or provide information about competitive organizations to any of the Organization’s tutors, contractors, tutee and tutee parents; or

d. Solicit, entice, or hire away other tutors & contractors of the Organization for the purpose of another opportunity that is in competition with the Organization.

Confidential Information
The Tutor acknowledges that, in any position the Tutor may hold, in and as a result of the Tutor’s agreement with the Organization, the Tutor will, or may, be making use of, acquiring or adding to information which is confidential to the Organization (the “Confidential Information”) and the Confidential Information is the exclusive property of the Organization. 
The Confidential Information will include all data and information relating to the operation and management of the Organization, including but not limited to, proprietary and trade secret system and accounting records to which access is obtained by the Tutor, including Work Product, Computer Software, Other Proprietary Data, Work Flow Operations, Marketing and Advertising, Contractor Information and Client/Tutee/Parent Information. 
The Confidential Information will also include any information that has been disclosed by a third party to the Organization and is governed by a non-disclosure agreement entered into between that third party and the Organization. 
The Confidential Information will not include information that:  a. Is generally known in the industry of the Organization;  b. Is now or subsequently becomes generally available to the public through no wrongful act of the Tutor;   c. Is independently created by the Tutor without direct or indirect use of the Confidential Information; or  d. The Tutor rightfully obtains from a third party who has the right to transfer or disclose it. 
The Confidential Information will also not include anything developed or produced by the Tutor during the Tutor’s term of service with the Organization, including but not limited to, any intellectual property, process, design, development, creation, research, invention, know-how, trade name, trade-mark or copyright that:  a. was developed without the use of equipment, supplies, facility or Confidential Information of the Organization;  b. was developed entirely on the Tutor’s own time;  c. does not result from any work performed by the Tutor for the Organization; and  d. does not relate to any actual or reasonably anticipated operation opportunity of the Organization.

23. Notwithstanding any other term or condition expressed or implied in this Agreement to the contrary, the Tutor will not have the authority to enter into any contracts or commitments for or on the behalf of the Organization without first obtaining the express written consent of the Organization.

Termination Due to Discontinuance of Operation 24. Notwithstanding any other term or condition expressed or implied in this Agreement, in the event that the Organization will discontinue operating its operation, then, at the Organization’s sole option, and as permitted by law, this Agreement will terminate within 24 hours in which the Organization ceases operations with the same force and effect as if it were originally set as the Termination Date of this Agreement.

Termination of Service
25. If the Tutor wishes to terminate his service with the Organization, the Tutor will provide the Organization with a written notice of agenda, four (4) weeks early from the desired date of termination.  
26. The Termination Date specified by the Tutor may expire on any day of the month and upon the Termination Date, the Organization will forthwith pay to the Tutor any outstanding portion of the Tutor’s compensation.

27. Once notice has been given by either party for any reason, the Tutor and the Organization agree to execute their duties and obligations under this Agreement diligently and in good faith through to the end of the notice period.
